    Achievement

    My interest in photography blossomed in middle school, and the desire to pursue it for a lifetime took root in my heart. The journey began with joining the yearbook team, participating in visual arts competitions, and hosting small exhibitions during my high school years. I also applied for an internship to a professional studio that equipped me with valuable insights into the intricacies of commercial photography, where I gained hands-on expertise in utilizing various lighting techniques, mastering the use of photography gear, and refining post-production skills through applications like Photoshop. The pivotal moment came when I was accepted into CalArts, solidifying my commitment to dive into the art industry.
